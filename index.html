Flip 7 Scoring Notes

This helper uses the official Flip 7: With a Vengeance scoring: add your number cards, apply ÷2 first if you have it, subtract any -2 to -10 modifiers, then add +15 if you completed a Flip 7.

When you choose card-based scoring in the grid, you can quickly mark which number cards, modifiers, and Flip 7 bonus you had, and the app will compute that round's score for you.
